About CKEditor 5, v0.1.0

This is the first developer preview of CKEditor 5.

After 2 years of work, building the next generation editor from scratch and closing over 400 tickets, we created a highly extensible and flexible architecture which consists of an amazing editing framework and editing solutions that will be built on top of it.

Notes

CKEditor 5 is under heavy development and this demo is not production-ready software.

It has bugs that we are aware of - and that we will be working on in the next few iterations of the project. Stay tuned for some updates soon!
